Cartesian Coordinate

This is the cartesian coordinate system. Suppose it is like a straight line with positve and negativa numbers called x, but now is intersected by another straight line with positive and negative numberswith 0 as the intersection called y.

The cartesian system is a plane where we can find points by using coordinates. The coordinates have the form (x,y). Example, locate the point (3,5) in the cartesian plane.

Explanation: (3,5) is the point that has a value of +3 in the x line and +5 in the y line. From the origin, anything to the left or down are negative numbers and anything from the right and to the top are the positive numbers.
