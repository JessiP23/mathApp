Some of questions are:

  1. Find the domain: The domain is in the interval [-2,2]
  2. Find the range: The range is in the interval [-4,4]
  3. What are the y intercepts: (0,4)
  4. What are the x intercepts: (-2,0) and (2,0)
  1. Find the interval of decrease: The interval is from [-2,-4] to (0,4)
  2. Find the interval where f is increasing: The interval is from (0,4) to [2,-4]

Those are some of the common questions you will be encountering in your precalculus class. Please remember what the y and x intercepts are. Thank you.
